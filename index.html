Our Story

Rooted in Family.
Built for Generations.

Banks Fresh Farms isn't just land — it's legacy. What started as a commitment to family, faith, and self-sufficiency has grown into a multi-generational operation grounded in purpose. Every step we take is connected to those who came before us and those we're building for next.

We don't just grow food — we cultivate responsibility, ownership, and community. From our soil to your table, everything we produce carries the weight of our name and the pride of our family.
